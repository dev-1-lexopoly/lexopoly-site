Getting Started

Start recovering lost revenue in under 5 minutes

1

Connect Your Clio Account

Click "Connect with Clio" and authorize RevenueRescue to access your calendar and activities. We use Clio's secure OAuth authentication, which means we never see or store your password.

Privacy Note: We only read your calendar data to find unbilled time. We never modify anything in Clio without your explicit approval.
2

Review Your Suggestions

RevenueRescue automatically scans your calendar for meetings, calls, and events that may not have been billed. You'll see suggestions in your queue showing:

  • The matter and client name
  • Event description from your calendar
  • Suggested duration and your billing rate
  • Estimated value to recover
Pro Tip: Review suggestions at the end of each day or week to keep your billing current.
3

Approve with One Click

For each suggestion, you have three options:

  • Approve — Posts the time entry directly to Clio
  • Edit — Modify details (description, duration, rate) before approving
  • Ignore — Skip items you don't want to bill

Approved entries appear in your Clio time sheet immediately. That's it — revenue captured!
