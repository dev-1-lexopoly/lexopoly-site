Getting Started

Start recovering lost revenue in under 5 minutes

1

Connect Your Clio Account

Click "Connect with Clio" and authorize RevenueRescue to access your calendar and activities. We only read your data to find unbilled time - we never modify anything without your approval.

2

Review Suggestions

RevenueRescue automatically scans your calendar for meetings, calls, and events that may not have been billed. You'll see suggestions in your queue with:

  • The matter and client name
  • Suggested duration and rate
  • Estimated value to recover
3

Approve with One Click

Review each suggestion and click "Approve" to post the time entry directly to Clio. You can also edit details before approving, or click "Ignore" for items you don't want to bill.
