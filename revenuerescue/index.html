Frequently Asked Questions

How does RevenueRescue detect unbilled time?

RevenueRescue connects to Clio using secure authentication and monitors four activity sources: calendar events, tasks, communications (emails and calls), and documents. It identifies client-related work and filters out internal/non-billable items. Machine intelligence analyzes each activity to generate appropriate billing suggestions—all in one unified queue.

Is my Clio data secure?

Yes, absolutely. We use secure authentication, which means we never see or store your Clio password. Your firm's data is kept completely separate from other firms.

What's the ROI for my firm?

Research shows law firms lose 10-20% of billable time to forgotten activities. For a solo practitioner billing $350/hour, RevenueRescue typically recovers $14K-$36K annually. Solo plan costs $1,788/year (or $1,430 with annual billing)—net benefit of $12,500-$34,500 (7-19× return). Larger firms see even greater returns. Payback period is typically 18-30 days. Do I need to change how I work?

No. RevenueRescue works invisibly in the background. You continue your day as normal—meeting with clients, making calls, sending emails. The system monitors your calendar automatically. At your convenience (typically end of day or week), you review suggestions and approve with one click. No workflow changes required.

Paid Subscription (from $149/month): Unlimited access to full calendar history. Real-time ongoing monitoring of all future activities. Complete detailed analytics and unlimited billing suggestions. Plans scale by attorney count—Solo, Firm, Practice, and Enterprise tiers available.

You can start your 7-day free trial immediately. The trial gives you access to RevenueRescue's core features using your last 30 days of calendar activity. What if I accidentally approve a non-billable suggestion?

RevenueRescue includes undo capability for recently approved entries. If you realize you approved something by mistake (like a personal appointment or internal meeting), simply click the undo button and that entry won't post to your Clio time sheet. You maintain complete control over every billable entry—mistakes are easy to fix.
