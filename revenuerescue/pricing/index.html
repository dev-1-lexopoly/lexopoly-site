Pricing Questions

How is the pricing justified for my firm?

Think of it as capturing a few hours per month of forgotten billable time. At $350/hour, capturing just 1-2 hours covers your subscription. At $600/hour (typical for partners), it takes even less. RevenueRescue pays for itself in the first week of each month. The rest is pure profit. Solo practitioners typically recover $12K-$34K net annually. Multi-attorney firms often recover $50K-$200K+ firm-wide.

What's included in each plan?

All plans include core features: Unlimited billing suggestions, unlimited calendar monitoring, Clio integration, AI narrative generation, revenue dashboard, and all future updates. Higher tiers add multi-attorney support, firm-wide analytics, role-based access, and priority support. No per-user fees - pricing is by attorney count only, with unlimited paralegals, staff, and admin users.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes! Save 20% with annual billing. Prepay for the year and get 2.4 months free. Solo plan: $1,432/year (vs $1,788 monthly). Firm plan: $2,870/year (vs $3,588 monthly). Email us for annual billing.

How does the trial work?

7-day trial with 30-day calendar window. When you start your trial, RevenueRescue analyzes your last 30 days of Clio calendar data and shows billing suggestions from recent activities. You'll see summary analytics and 3 anonymized sample opportunities to understand the potential. Credit card required to secure your trial. You won't be charged during the 7-day period. If you cancel within 7 days, you're never charged. After 7 days, billing automatically starts at $149/month for unlimited access to full history and ongoing monitoring.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit cards via Stripe (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover). Payments are processed securely and PCI-compliant. Monthly billing standard. Annual prepayment option available (10% discount). No checks or wire transfers at this time.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no questions asked. Cancel from your account settings anytime. Access continues until end of current billing period, then auto-renewal stops. We're confident in the value - if RevenueRescue isn't recovering revenue for you, we don't want your money.

How does multi-attorney pricing work?

We price by attorney count, not users. Your plan tier is based on how many attorneys bill time: Solo (1 attorney) $149/mo, Firm (2-10 attorneys) $299/mo, Practice (11-25 attorneys) $499/mo, Enterprise (25+) custom pricing. Add unlimited paralegals, legal assistants, and admin staff at no extra cost. If your firm grows, you simply upgrade to the next tier.

Is there a setup fee or onboarding cost?

No. Setup is free and takes 2 minutes. You connect your Clio account via OAuth (click a button), RevenueRescue requests calendar access, you approve, done. No technical setup, no data migration, no IT involvement required. First suggestions appear within 24 hours of connecting.

What if I don't use Clio?

RevenueRescue currently requires Clio Manage. We chose Clio for initial launch because it's the market leader for small/mid-size law firms (70%+ market share). Other practice management systems coming in 2025: MyCase (Q2), PracticePanther (Q3), Smokeball (Q4). If you use a different system, contact us - we prioritize integrations based on customer demand.

What's your refund policy?

14-day money-back guarantee (after trial). If you're not satisfied in the first 14 days after trial ends, contact support for full refund. After 14 days, no refunds (but you can cancel anytime and access continues until period ends). We want happy customers who are capturing real revenue - if it's not working for you, we'll refund you.
