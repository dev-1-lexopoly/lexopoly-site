1. Overview

Lexopoly LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates three products: our marketing website (lexopoly.com), LocalTranscribe (desktop transcription software), and QuoteCreator (mobile estimate app). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information across all our products and services.

Privacy-First Design

All products built with privacy as a core principle, not an afterthought.

GDPR Compliant

Full compliance with EU data protection regulations across all products.

Minimal Data Collection

We only collect what's essential for functionality and service delivery.

No Data Selling

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

üìä Website (lexopoly.com) Privacy Practices

What We Collect

Analytics & Tracking

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4): We use GA4 to understand how visitors use our website. This includes page views, session duration, and navigation patterns.
  • UTM Tracking Parameters: We track campaign attribution (utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign, utm_content, utm_term) to measure marketing effectiveness.
  • IP Addresses: Automatically anonymized in GA4 for privacy protection.
  • First-Touch & Last-Touch Attribution: We store attribution data in your browser (localStorage/sessionStorage) to understand conversion paths.

Form Submissions

When you submit forms on lexopoly.com, we collect:

  • Contact Form: Name, email, message content (via Formspree)
  • Demo Request: Name, email, company (optional), message (via Formspree)
  • Signup Form: Name, email, intended use (via Formspree)

What We Don't Collect

  • No advertising tracking pixels or third-party ad networks
  • No cross-site tracking cookies
  • No sale of visitor data to data brokers
  • No personally identifiable information without explicit consent

Cookie Consent

GDPR-Compliant Cookie Consent

We use essential cookies for analytics and attribution tracking only. You can decline cookies, and our website will still function properly. Cookie consent is required before any tracking begins.

Third-Party Services

  • Formspree: Form submission handling (contact, demo, signup forms)
  • Google Analytics 4: Website analytics with IP anonymization
  • GitHub Pages: Website hosting and content delivery

üîí Core Privacy Principle: LOCAL PROCESSING

LocalTranscribe processes all audio and transcription data locally on your computer. Your sensitive conversations, legal discussions, and confidential audio never travel to our servers or any cloud service.

What We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (required for account access)
  • Name (for billing and customer service)
  • Company information (optional, for business accounts)
  • Password (encrypted and stored securely)

Billing Information

  • Billing address
  • Payment method information (processed by Stripe)
  • Transaction history and invoices

Usage Analytics (Anonymized)

  • Number of transcriptions processed (not the content)
  • Software version and performance metrics
  • Feature usage patterns (which features are used, not what data is processed)
  • Error logs and crash reports (containing no personal audio data)

What We DON'T Collect

  • ‚ùå Audio files - never uploaded to our servers
  • ‚ùå Transcription content - stays on your device
  • ‚ùå Sensitive professional data (legal, medical, journalistic)
  • ‚ùå Client information from your transcriptions

Professional Privacy Benefits

  • Attorney-Client Privilege: Protected by design - no cloud exposure risk
  • HIPAA Compliance Support: Local processing supports healthcare privacy requirements
  • Confidential Sources: Journalists can protect source identity completely
  • Government Security: Meets strict data handling requirements

Data Security

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) for all account data transmission
  • Encryption at rest for stored account data
  • Secure password hashing using industry best practices
  • Multi-factor authentication options

5. Shared Privacy Practices

The following practices apply to all Lexopoly products:

5.1 GDPR Compliance

  • Full compliance with EU General Data Protection Regulation
  • Data minimization - we only collect what's necessary
  • Lawful basis for processing (consent, contract, legitimate interest)
  • Regular privacy impact assessments

5.2 Data Security Standards

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) for all data transmission
  • Encryption at rest for stored data
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt/Argon2)
  • Multi-factor authentication support

5.3 No Data Selling

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your data is yours, not a product.

5.4 Limited Third-Party Sharing

We only share data with essential service providers:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe, Google Play, Apple App Store (for billing only)
  • Email Service: Transactional emails only (account notifications, support)
  • Analytics: Google Analytics 4 (website only, with IP anonymization)
  • Form Handling: Formspree (website contact/demo/signup forms)

6. Your Rights

You have comprehensive control over your data across all Lexopoly products:

6.1 Access Your Data

  • View all data we have about you
  • Download your data in portable format (JSON/CSV)
  • Request detailed data reports

6.2 Delete Your Data

  • Delete your account at any time
  • Request deletion of specific information
  • Cancel subscriptions with no penalty
  • 30-day data retention after account deletion (for dispute resolution)

6.3 Export Your Information

  • Export account data in machine-readable format
  • Port your data to other services
  • LocalTranscribe: All transcription data already on your device
  • QuoteCreator: Export quotes as PDF, CSV, or JSON

6.4 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you're in the European Union, you also have the right to:

  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Restrict processing under certain circumstances
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Data portability (export in structured format)

Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@lexopoly.com or use the data controls in your account settings. We'll respond within 30 days.
